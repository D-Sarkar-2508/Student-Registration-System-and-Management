EST. 2005

DS College of Engineering & Technology

DS College of Engineering & Technology is a premier technical institution located in Behala, Kolkata, West Bengal. We are committed to providing quality education in engineering and technology disciplines to shape the future leaders of India.

Our state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry-connected curriculum ensure that every student graduates with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills ready for the modern workplace.

We are affiliated with the West Bengal University of Technology (MAKAUT) and approved by AICTE, maintaining the highest standards of technical education.
